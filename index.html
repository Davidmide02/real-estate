How I Lost All my Investments in Real Estate

Learn the exact secrets used by the 1% real estate billionaires to amass wealth through real estate investing

16 years ago, I was a young ambitious woman who survived through school and being from a not so privileged background. I had to combine school with helping mum with trades to pay my fees as I have always wanted to be more and a blessing.

I got out of the University with high hopes of securing my dream job and finally living the life I had envisaged. However, after years of fruitless search for a job, I finally got into the business of hawking used clothes (okrika), with the sum of 3,600 naira (about $10).

After many months of doing this business, I made just enough money to take care of my needs but not enough money to become and make the kind of impact I have always desired. I needed a change in my life, and I needed it fast.

On a sunny afternoon, I came across an old school friend, it was very obvious she was on a very different financial level. Being curious, I immediately asked what the source was, it was real estate. We spent the rest of the afternoon discussing the opportunities in Real Estate and how I might join the industry. She showed me the great benefit of investing in real estate, and it finally dawned on me that my life could change forever if I played my cards right

    BUT I HAD SOME FEARS!!!
  • Fake products and scams
  • High cost of entry for genuine real estate product with high returns
  • Integrity issues with developers and other real estate companies
